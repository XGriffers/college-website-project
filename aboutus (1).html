About the Rose and the Raven Inn

  • About Us

The owner of the inn Mathias Connor was from the port of Ventarco, a goblin society with a
less than stable economy. Being from a harbor, Mathias grew up owning his own boat, sailing the
seven seas and stopping at any landmass that looked inviting to peruse the local eateries.
At the age of 49, Mathias decided to retire from adventuring and settle down with Gwen Lula,
a half-elf farm girl he met during his travels. It was then that Mathias decided to share his
travels with fellow tourists and anyone else who wanders into the establishment.

Roaming the countryside, Mathias and Gwen stumbled upon an abandoned log cabin and
decided to turn it into the inn known as the rose and the raven. The inn itself is lit solely by
candlelight with wrought iron sconces on the massive oak beams supporting the upper floors
with a fireplace along the wall.

The Inn owned and operated by Mathias who primarily runs the bar and kitchen, along with
the many waitresses he employs serves a variety of meals, from fish and bread to stews of all kinds.
The atmosphere is friendly, and there is typically live entertainment every day. The bar and kitchen
are open daily from sun up to sun down; there are a wide variety of rooms
available with the most lavish of them starting at a dollar per night. The rooms themselves
feature a large window overlooking the golden wheat fields and pristine river in the distance
and a wardrobe to store belongings for the night.
