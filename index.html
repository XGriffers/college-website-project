Welcome to The Rose and the Raven Inn

Welcome to The Rose and the Raven Inn, a log cabin situated along a dusty and secluded road.
Inside there is joyful music and a charming and friendly atmosphere. The inn itself
features darkened red drapes over the windows, massive oak pillars supporting the upper floor,
which houses the inn's hotel-like features, the inn lit with the warming glow of candles
in masterfully crafted wrought iron sconces and chandeliers, and a cozy hearth. Inside there
are also depictions of the now bar keep's past adventures. There is also plenty of seating available to accommodate the largest of parties.

We offer hot food and frothy ale, rooms at reasonable prices, and live entertainment nearly every night.
The staff is hard-working and friendly, so feel free to stop by for a pint.
