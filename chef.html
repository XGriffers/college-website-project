Meet Matthias Connor

Matthias Connor, the adventurer, owner of the inn and the chef has travelled the seas and
along the way picked up on islandic delicacies from far and wide.

Being that Matthias left home at a young age to sail the seas on a ship of his own,
Mr. Connor never had a formal education, instead relied heavily on the experience of
new found friends to show him how to prepare the wide varity of dishes to keep himself
and his crew fed. Armed with his travel journal and a sharp knife, Matthias now aims to
take you on a journey of flavors.
